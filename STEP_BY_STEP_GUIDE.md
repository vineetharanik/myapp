## 📄 **STEP 4: SETUP OVERLEAF PAPER (2 minutes)**

### **A. Create Overleaf Project**
1. **Go to**: https://www.overleaf.com
2. **Signup**: Free account
3. **Create**: "New Project" → "Blank Project" → Name: "DevBalance AI Paper"

### **B. Add Paper Content**
1. **Delete** default `main.tex`
2. **Create** `ieee_accurate_paper.tex`
3. **Copy** all content from `ieee_accurate_paper.tex`
4. **Paste** into Overleaf
5. **Click** "Recompile"

### **C. Upload All Images**
1. **Click** "Files" (left menu)
2. **Click** "Upload"
3. **Upload** ALL files:
   - `accurate_system_architecture.png`
   - `accurate_workflow.png`
   - `user_engagement.png`
   - `mental_health_improvement.png`
   - `system_comparison.png`
